Coffee Companion

The coffee app to help you brew the best coffee you can!

Use Coffee Companion to determine how much water is needed for your next pour over brew.

Coffee Companion is an open sourced iOS app built with Swift and SwiftUI. It is being built in the open. Every Monday at 1:00pm CST, Maegan from Apps by MW goes live and works on another part of the app. You can watch live at Twitch.tv/appsbymw and if you miss it, then you can watch it again on YouTube.com.
